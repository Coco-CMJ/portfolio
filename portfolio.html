Whispering Cats

Whispering Cats is a cozy clicker game designed to promote calmness and emotional well-being. Built around the concept of mindfulness and gentle discovery, the game invites players to interact with and collect unique cats — each symbolizing a different aspect of mental health and self-care. Through soothing visuals, soft ambient sound, and relaxing gameplay, players gradually learn simple mental health insights as they unlock new feline companions. Each cat comes with its own personality, story, and “whisper” — a small piece of reflection or advice that connects emotional healing with everyday kindness and awareness.

Gameplay & Key Features

The game was inspired by the UN’s Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being, with the goal of raising mental health awareness in a gentle, accessible way. Rather than presenting clinical information, Whispering Cats integrates emotional learning into small, comforting interactions — encouraging players to take breaks, breathe, and care for themselves.

Gameplay

Whispering Cats combines simple clicker mechanics with gentle storytelling. Players earn affection by tapping and interacting with cats, which can then be used to unlock new feline companions, upgrades, and decorations.

Each time a new cat is discovered, players unlock a short story and a “Whisper” — a piece of mental health insight or self-care reminder tied to the cat’s personality. Over time, these collected Whispers form a gentle guide to emotional well-being.
