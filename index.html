About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Coco Chong, a 25-year-old Multimedia Designer born at Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia. With a background in both Fine Arts and Multimedia Design, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for expressive color, emotional storytelling, and artistic diversity.

I began my creative journey with a Diploma in Fine Arts at The One Academy, where I explored various traditional mediums and artistic styles. Later, I pursued a BA (Hons) in Multimedia Design at SEGi University, expanding my skills into digital design, UI/UX, and motion graphics.

I love blending the emotion and texture of traditional art with the structure and interactivity of design. Through my work, I aim to create visuals that not only look beautiful — but feel meaningful.
